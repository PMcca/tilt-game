-Tilt Game Prototype-

A physics based puzzle game where the aim is to use buttons at the bottom of the screen to control contraptions which guide the ball to its goal. Use the stars to create an extra boost in your desired direction. Reach the flag to win.

Red = Trap Door

Cyan = Moving Platform

Yellow = Elevator

Brown = Boost (left and right respectively)
